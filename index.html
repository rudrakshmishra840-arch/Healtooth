Frequently Asked Questions

How often should I visit the dentist?

We recommend visiting every 6 months for regular check-ups and cleanings. Dr. Khatri will recommend a schedule based on your individual needs.

Is teeth whitening safe?

Yes! Professional teeth whitening done by Dr. Khatri is safe and effective. We use clinically-proven methods and precautions to protect your gums.

Do you offer emergency dental care?
